Most housing societies still run on paper, WhatsApp forwards, spreadsheets, and manual payment entries.

A small incident at a police station made me rethink how this actually works.

I was there for passport verification, and the officer asked for a maintenance bill from my building, ideally something from about a year ago.

The problem?

Our society still distributes printed maintenance bills every month, individually stamped and manually distributed.

They usually arrive folded into newspapers or slipped under the door… and eventually disappear into a pile of other papers.

So when we actually needed one, it was impossible to find.

That moment made me think about something: Why is a **monthly process like society maintenance billing still so manual?**

In most communities:

- Notices are printed and often missed
- Complaints are handled informally
- Payments are tracked manually
- Late fees happen simply because people forget

And when you scale this to 150+ flats… it quickly becomes tedious and inefficient.

For something that happens **every single month**, the system feels surprisingly outdated.

This isn’t just a housing society problem.

It applies to:

- Residential societies
- Commercial complexes
- Campus hostels / shared communities
- Basically any community with recurring billing + a payment system

That got me thinking:

What if this worked like a **simple SaaS platform instead?**

Since this is a **recurring monthly workflow**, I wanted to design it with that in mind and not as a one-off solution for a single society, but as a modular system that can scale across different types of communities.

So I started building **FlatMate :** a system for managing communities and their day-to-day whereabouts. 

[logo image]

The idea is simple:

Residents get a dashboard where they can:

- see notices
- register complaints
- view invoices
- pay maintenance online

Admins get tools to:

- manage residents and complaints
- track payments and apply late fees
- generate invoices from the database
- automatically send invoices as PDFs via email with a single click

This is a work-in-progress, and I’m documenting the journey as I build.
